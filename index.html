Arch Linux Hyprland Rice

A custom Hyprland rice with a matugen-based dynamic theming engine. A key-bound wallpaper switcher feeds into matugen, which extracts the active wallpaper's color palette and applies it live to Waybar; the entire theme shifts with every wallpaper change.
